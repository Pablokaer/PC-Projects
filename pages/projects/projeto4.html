About the Project

The Tutorial Channel is a video content creation project focused on web development, productivity tools, and tech careers. Each video is scripted, recorded, and edited with special attention to clarity, pacing, and audio quality — the factors that most impact viewer retention.


The production pipeline goes from screen capture with OBS Studio to editing and color grading in DaVinci Resolve, through thumbnail and artwork creation in Canva. The channel's SEO is structured with optimized titles, detailed descriptions, and end cards to maximize organic reach.
